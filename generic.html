Welcome to my portfolio website, hopefully this will help you see my work and understand more about me.

Hi my name is Dak Wiyual, I am currently 26 years old with a goal of becoming a fullstack web developer. I have been studing coding for about a year and a half now. I first got interested in coding while I was working my current job as a janitor. We have to clock in to work with an old fashioned time clock, and one day I had to manually calulate the time cards for payroll and it took forever, so I wanted to create an application that could read the timecards with A.I and calcutate it do it for me. So I learned python from a site I found online but then after I learned the basics of that I wonder what can I do with it and thats when it all started. I never created that app but it has led me down a incredible path of learning hopefully toward a new career.

Currenty I am enrolled in Lambda School's fullstack Web Development Course. We cover many concepts in Lambda, such as hmtl, css, less, and Javascript; but soon we will be learning React, Node, Algorithms, and basically we will be learning how to develop a web application from front-end to back-end. Throughout my time in Lambda I wil have the opportunity to work on projects and I plan on updating them to this portfolio website to show my progress and also my skillset as I progress as a developer.
