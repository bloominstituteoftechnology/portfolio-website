My Story:

I grew up in the beautiful state of Colorado and fell in love with all things outdoors. In 2017, while finishing my psychology degree online, I moved to Atlanta, GA, where I currently reside and continue to find the beauty in the world.

Growing up, I was greatly influenced by my father and older brothers. My father was a senior Java developer for the DoD for 15 years and my brothers have shown talents in software engineering from young ages. They're work was always interesting to me, but it felt like a 'boys' feild, and since I don't have a strong math background, that I should focus on something else. I graduated from Colorado State University in August 2018 with a bachelor's degree in psychology and while I will very likely never work as a lab assistant or social worker, I am very grateful for the skills and knowledge that I learned while pursuing this degree and believe they are skills that I apply to my daily life and future careers.

After moving to Atlanta, I future surrounded myself with those in the tech industry: I worked as a nanny for my cousin who owns a tech start-up. After confessing my interest in the field, he pointed me towards Lambda School and have since fallen in love with software engineering.
