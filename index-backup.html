Print

Web Dev is definitely my bread and butter, but print is also a passion of mine. Editing, proofing, layout and even some graphics design for the cover - not only is the work fun, it's also rewarding! There's nothing like the feeling of holding a book you made yourself in your hands, and you can perform a valuable service to others as well by spreading knowledge. Click learn more to see a sample of my work.

Community

When communities are strong, everyone benefits. Health improves, productivity increases, and cooperation reigns. This principle of collaboration and sharing my labor with both my family and my community is a cornerstone of my work ethic. "It takes a village to raise a child" applies to development projects too! Click learn more to read about some of my non-profit and community work.

Media

Many of my essays have appeared in print or have been published online. I typically write on topics regarding the intersection of politics, technology, and society; though I'm often asked to write press releases as well.
