The Pig Game

This is probably my most favorite and difficult project thus far. Using Html, Css, and JavaScript I was able to create this Pig Dice Game. The players can roll as many times as they wish; however, if they roll a 1 they will lose their current score & it will be the other's player turn. The players can hold and whatever they had in their current score, it will be added to their global score. The first player to reach to 100 points wins the game.
