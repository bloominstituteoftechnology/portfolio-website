Andante

Hello everyone my name is Ramonta Lee and I am happy that you have made it to my page. I am currently on the never ending journey to becoming a web developer and software engineer. I have spent over 9yrs in the field of music education and bring with me to this field a passion for not only creativity but, analytical and reflective thinking. My primary instrument is the viola and I have been playing for over 20yrs getting to perform all over the country, and with major headlining talent. These experiences feed me and are what drives my interest in the ability to create and collaborate with others to design beautiful creations on the web. I am a military veteran through which I was to experience other cultures and perspectives as well as know what it means to be of service to others. I hope you enjoy getting to know me and feel free to reach out.
